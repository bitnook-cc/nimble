The **Nimble RPG Vault** is your comprehensive digital repository for everything related to the Nimble tabletop role-playing game system. Whether you're a new player getting started or an experienced GM crafting epic adventures, this vault contains the tools and resources you need.

## What is Nimble?

Nimble is a modern tabletop RPG system designed for **fast play, heroic action, and collaborative storytelling**. Built with both new and experienced players in mind, Nimble emphasizes:

- **Quick Setup**: Get playing in minutes, not hours
- **Heroic Fantasy**: Characters are competent heroes from level 1
- **Streamlined Rules**: Easy to learn, hard to master
- **Flexible System**: Perfect for one-shots or epic campaigns
