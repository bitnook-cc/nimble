Hearth & Home

*Tier 3 Fire Spell*

**Casting Time:** 10 minutes.
**Effect:** Conjure a welcoming inn, complete with sturdy wooden tables, plush chairs, and a soft rug underfoot. At its heart burns a cozy fire in a fireplace, filling the space with warmth and light. The inn lasts for 12 hours and vanishes without a trace afterward.

> Why is this secret?
> Safe resting anywhere is incredibly powerful. This spell is notoriously tricky to cast manually, so it is most often imbued in wands for wealthy travelers. Allow it cautiously, as it can easily break game balance.
