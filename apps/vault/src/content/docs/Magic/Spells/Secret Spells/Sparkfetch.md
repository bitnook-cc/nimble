# Sparkfetch


*Cantrip Lightning Spell*

**Effect:** Loudly teleport a tiny, unheld metal item you can see to yourself.

> Why is this secret?
> This seemingly harmless cantrip led to a wave of mysterious thefts, as stormy nights became prime time for mischievous first-year students pilfering coins and valuables. Quickly banned, it remains an infamous spell in magical academies.
