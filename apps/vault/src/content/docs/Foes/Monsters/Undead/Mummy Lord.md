# Mummy Lord


**Level:** 21 | **Size:** Medium | **Group:** Undead
**HP:** 280 | **Armor:** None | **Speed:** 6

**Passive**
* **Cursed Gaze.** When crit: DC 20 INT save, or suffer 1 Wound.

**Actions**
* **Scarab Swarm.** Summon 10 scarab minions (d6) within 6 spaces. Then:
* **Slam (2×).** 1d4+20. On damage: Dazed.
