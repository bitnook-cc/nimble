# Advanced Combat Rules

Learn the advanced combat mechanics that separate novices from masters in the Nimble system.

## Flanking and Positioning

When you and an ally are on opposite sides of an enemy, you both gain advantage on your attacks.

## Opportunity Attacks

Leaving an enemy's melee range triggers an opportunity attack unless you use the Disengage action.

## Cover System

- **Half Cover**: +2 to AC and Dexterity saves
- **Three-quarters Cover**: +5 to AC and Dexterity saves
- **Full Cover**: Cannot be directly targeted
