# Complete Classes Compendium

The definitive guide to all twelve core classes in Nimble.

## Warriors

### Fighter
Masters of martial combat, fighters excel at both offense and defense.

### Barbarian
Rage-fueled warriors who trade defense for devastating attacks.

## Spellcasters

### Wizard
Academic mages who learn spells through study and preparation.

### Sorcerer
Natural-born magic users with innate power flowing through their veins.

## Hybrid Classes

### Paladin
Holy warriors who blend martial prowess with divine magic.

### Ranger
Skilled hunters who combine archery, tracking, and nature magic.
